## Article

NEW YORK, March 10, 2026 -- Zeta Network Group announced that it entered into a securities purchase agreement with certain institutional investors to issue and sell up to $10 million of Senior 10% Original Issue Discount Convertible Promissory Notes together with warrants to purchase Class A ordinary shares.

The release states that the first closing is expected to occur on or about March 12, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions. It also states that a second closing for the remainder of the notes and warrants is expected no sooner than May 12, 2026, and is subject to the closing conditions contained in the purchase agreement.

The notes are described as convertible immediately upon issuance, subject to certain exceptions, into ordinary shares at a variable conversion price subject to a floor price. The warrants are described as exercisable immediately upon issuance, subject to certain exceptions, and expiring five years from the initial exercise date.

This is a mixed registered direct structure with staged closings, immediate convertibility and exercisability language, and future optional supply tied to notes and warrants rather than a plain common-stock-only offering.
