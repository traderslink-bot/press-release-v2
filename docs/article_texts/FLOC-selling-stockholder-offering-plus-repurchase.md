## Article

Flowco Holdings Inc. announced the pricing of an underwritten public offering by certain affiliates of GEC Advisors LLC, as the selling stockholders, of 7,800,000 shares of Flowco's Class A common stock at a public offering price of $22.00 per share.

The Selling Stockholders also granted the underwriters a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional 1,170,000 shares. The offering is expected to close on March 23, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions.

Flowco is not issuing or selling any shares in the offering and will not receive any proceeds from the shares sold by the Selling Stockholders.

Flowco also announced that it intends to purchase from the underwriters 780,000 shares that are the subject of the offering. The offering is not conditioned upon the closing of the share repurchase, but the share repurchase is conditioned upon the closing of the offering.

The offering is being made pursuant to a registration statement declared effective by the SEC and only by means of a prospectus supplement and accompanying prospectus.
