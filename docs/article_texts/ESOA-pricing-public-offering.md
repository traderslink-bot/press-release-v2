## Article

HUNTINGTON, W.Va., Feb. 19, 2026 -- Energy Services of America Corporation announced the pricing of an underwritten public offering of 1,740,000 shares of common stock at a public offering price of $11.50 per share.

The release states that the aggregate gross proceeds from the offering are expected to be approximately $20.0 million before underwriting discounts, commissions, and offering expenses. It also states that the underwriter received a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional 261,000 shares of common stock.

The article says the offering is expected to close on or about February 20, 2026, subject to the satisfaction of customary closing conditions. Assuming full exercise of the underwriter option, gross proceeds would increase to approximately $23.0 million before deductions.

This is the pricing-stage follow-up to the earlier proposal and gives a clear expected close date for the public offering.
