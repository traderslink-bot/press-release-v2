## Article

LONDON and NEW YORK, Feb. 18, 2026 -- Compass Pathways plc announced the pricing of its public offering of 17,500,000 American Depositary Shares at a public offering price of $8.00 per ADS, and in lieu of ADSs to certain institutional investors, pre-funded warrants to purchase up to 1,250,000 ADSs at a public offering price of $7.9999 per pre-funded warrant.

The release states that Compass Pathways granted the underwriters a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional 2,812,500 ADSs at the public offering price, less underwriting discounts and commissions. It also states that all securities are to be sold by Compass Pathways.

The article says the offering is expected to close on or about February 20, 2026, subject to the satisfaction of customary closing conditions. Gross proceeds are expected to be $150.0 million before underwriting discounts, commissions, and other offering expenses.

This is the pricing-stage follow-up to the earlier proposal and gives a clear expected close date for a mixed ADS and pre-funded-warrant structure.
