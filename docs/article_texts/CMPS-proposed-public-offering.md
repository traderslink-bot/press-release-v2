## Article

LONDON and NEW YORK, Feb. 17, 2026 -- Compass Pathways plc announced the launch of a proposed public offering of $150.0 million of American Depositary Shares, and in lieu of ADSs to certain institutional investors, pre-funded warrants to purchase ADSs.

The release states that all securities are being offered by Compass Pathways and that the Company expects to grant the underwriters a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional $22.5 million of ADSs at the public offering price, less underwriting discounts and commissions. It also states that the proposed offering is subject to market and other conditions, and that there can be no assurance as to whether or when the proposed offering may be completed, or as to the actual size or terms of the proposed offering.

The article says the securities are being offered pursuant to an effective shelf registration statement on Form S-3.

This is a clean proposed mixed-structure offering with no final pricing or close date yet, so timing remains undetermined until a pricing update appears.
