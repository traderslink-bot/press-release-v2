## Article

FAIRBANKS, Alaska, Feb. 11, 2026 -- Contango ORE, Inc. announced the pricing of an underwritten offering of common stock consisting of 1,678,206 shares at an offering price of $24.96 per share to two institutional investors.

The release states that Contango also offered pre-funded warrants to purchase 325,000 shares at a purchase price of $24.95 per share, which equals the offering price per share less the $0.01 exercise price of each pre-funded warrant. It also states that aggregate gross proceeds from the offering will be approximately $50 million before underwriting discounts, commissions, and other offering expenses, and excluding the exercise of the pre-funded warrants.

The article says the closing of the offering is expected to occur on or about February 12, 2026, subject to the satisfaction of customary closing conditions. It further states that the Company intends to use most of the net proceeds to buy back gold hedge contracts and purchase gold put contracts for downside protection, with any remaining proceeds used for general corporate purposes, including working capital.

This is a priced mixed-structure offering with a clear near-term close date and a pre-funded-warrant component rather than a common-stock-only sale.
