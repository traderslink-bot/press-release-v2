## Article

REHOVOT, Israel, Feb. 5, 2026 -- CollPlant Biotechnologies announced that it entered into a definitive agreement for the issuance and sale of an aggregate of 1,600,000 ordinary shares at a purchase price of $1.25 per share in a registered direct offering.

The release states that, in a concurrent private placement, the Company will issue unregistered Series A warrants and unregistered Series B warrants to purchase up to 1,600,000 ordinary shares each. The Series A warrants are described as exercisable immediately and expiring five years after the effective date of a registration statement registering the underlying shares, while the Series B warrants are described as exercisable immediately and expiring eighteen months after the effective date of a registration statement registering the underlying shares.

The article says the offering is expected to close on or about February 6, 2026, subject to the satisfaction of customary closing conditions. It further states that the ordinary shares, but not the concurrent private placement warrants or the ordinary shares underlying those warrants, are being offered pursuant to an effective Form F-3 shelf registration statement.

This is a mixed registered direct plus concurrent private-warrant structure with a clear expected close date and a later registration-linked resale and exercise path for the warrant-related supply.
