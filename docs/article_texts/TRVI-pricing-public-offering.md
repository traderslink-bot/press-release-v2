## Article

NEW HAVEN, Conn., April 17, 2026 -- Trevi Therapeutics, Inc. announced the pricing of an underwritten public offering of 10,256,411 shares of its common stock at a public offering price of $3.90 per share.

The Company also granted the underwriters a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional 1,538,461 shares of common stock. The aggregate gross proceeds to the Company from the offering are expected to be approximately $40.0 million before deducting underwriting discounts and commissions and other offering expenses.

The offering is expected to close on or about April 20, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions.

The Company intends to use the net proceeds from the offering to fund research and development, commercial preparation activities, working capital and general corporate purposes.

The offering is being made pursuant to an effective shelf registration statement and a final prospectus supplement will be filed with the SEC.
