## Article

LOS ANGELES, Feb. 12, 2026 -- Cineverse Corp. announced a proposed underwritten public offering of shares of its Class A common stock.

The release states that Cineverse intends to grant the underwriter a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional 15% of the number of shares sold in the offering. It also states that the offering is subject to market conditions and that there can be no assurance as to whether, or when, the offering may be completed or as to the actual size or terms of the proposed offering.

The article says the offering is being made pursuant to an effective shelf registration statement on Form S-3 and will be made only by means of a prospectus supplement and accompanying prospectus.

This is a proposal-stage public offering with no firm pricing or closing date, so the timing remains uncertain until a later pricing or closing update appears.
