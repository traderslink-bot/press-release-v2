## Article

BEIJING, Feb. 27, 2026 -- VNET Group, Inc. announced a private placement of 81.0 million newly issued Class A ordinary shares to certain institutional investors at an offering price of US$1.7 per placement share, or US$10.2 per ADS.

The release states that the Company expects to close the private placement on or about March 3, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions. It also states that gross proceeds are anticipated to be approximately US$137.7 million before deducting placement agent fees and offering expenses.

The article says the securities are being offered and sold in a transaction not involving a public offering and in reliance on Regulation S of the Securities Act. It adds that the securities have not been registered under the Securities Act or applicable state securities laws and may not be reoffered or resold in the United States or to a U.S. person without registration or an applicable exemption.

This is a large private placement with a stated expected close date, but the resale path remains restricted because the securities are unregistered and sold in a private transaction.
