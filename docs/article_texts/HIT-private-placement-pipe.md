## Article

STUART, Fla., March 25, 2026 -- Health In Tech, Inc. announced that it entered into a securities purchase agreement for a private investment in public equity financing expected to result in gross proceeds of approximately $7.0 million before fees and expenses.

The release states that the PIPE is expected to close on or about March 27, 2026, subject to the satisfaction of customary closing conditions. It also states that, at the closing of the PIPE, the Company will issue an aggregate of 5,600,000 shares of Class A common stock at a price of $1.25 per share.

The article says the securities are being offered in a private placement under Section 4(a)(2) of the Securities Act and or Regulation D, and that the securities have not been registered under the Securities Act or applicable state securities laws. It adds that the securities may not be offered or sold in the United States except pursuant to an effective registration statement or an applicable exemption.

This is a clean private placement expected-close case where the financing may close shortly, but public resale still depends on a registration path or other exemption.
