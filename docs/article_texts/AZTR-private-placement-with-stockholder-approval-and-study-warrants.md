## Article

BRANFORD, Conn., March 19, 2026 -- Azitra, Inc. announced a private placement financing for gross proceeds of up to approximately $31.4 million, consisting of initial gross proceeds of approximately $10.5 million and up to an additional approximately $20.9 million upon potential future cash exercise of accompanying warrants at the election of investors.

The release states that the transaction is expected to close on or about March 20, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions. It also says the Company is selling Series A convertible preferred stock together with Series B and Series C warrants, with each warrant carrying a $0.123 exercise price and with pre-funded warrants potentially issuable in certain circumstances.

The release further states that the Series A preferred stock will automatically convert into common stock only upon stockholder approval and subject to beneficial ownership limits. It adds that the warrants become exercisable following stockholder approval, that the Series B warrants terminate 18 months after stockholder approval, and that the Series C warrants terminate, subject to certain exceptions, on the 30th calendar day following public announcement of data from the planned human cosmetic study. The article also states that the securities were issued in a transaction exempt from registration and that the Company must register the resale of the common shares issuable upon conversion and warrant exercise.

This creates an immediate financing close path plus a later gated supply path that depends on stockholder approval, resale registration, and future study-related warrant timing.
