## Article

NEW YORK, Feb. 26, 2026 -- The Bank of New York Mellon Corporation announced the pricing of a public offering of depositary shares representing interests in its preferred stock, with aggregate gross proceeds of $500 million.

The release states that the securities offered are depositary shares representing fractional interests in a series of preferred stock rather than common stock. It also indicates that the transaction is a priced capital markets offering with final terms already set.

This is an unusual preferred-stock financing structure and should be kept separate from ordinary common-share dilution cases.
