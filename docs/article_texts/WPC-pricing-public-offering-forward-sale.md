## Article

NEW YORK, Feb. 17, 2026 -- W. P. Carey Inc. announced the pricing of an underwritten public offering of 6,000,000 shares of common stock, offered on a forward basis in connection with forward sale agreements, for gross proceeds of $432 million.

The release states that the underwriters were granted a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional 900,000 shares of common stock. It also states that the Company intends to use the net proceeds, if any, received upon settlement of the forward sale agreements, or from any shares sold in lieu of the forward purchasers selling shares to the underwriters, to fund future investments, repay indebtedness, and for general corporate purposes.

This is a priced follow-on structure with forward-sale mechanics, which makes it different from a simple same-day common stock issuance even though pricing is already set.
