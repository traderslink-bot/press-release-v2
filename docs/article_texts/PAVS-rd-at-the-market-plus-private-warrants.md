Structure hint: registered direct offering plus concurrent private placement

## Article

Paranovus Entertainment Technology Limited announced a registered direct offering priced at-the-market under Nasdaq Rules for 14,285,715 Class A ordinary shares or equivalents at $0.35 per share.

Gross proceeds are expected to be approximately $5 million before fees and expenses.

The offering is expected to close on or about March 25, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions.

The shares are being offered from an effective Form F-3 shelf registration statement. The release also states that warrants will be issued in a concurrent private placement and that those securities may not be offered or sold in the United States absent an effective registration statement or an applicable exemption.
