## Article

NEW YORK, Feb. 12, 2026 -- Cineverse Corp. announced the pricing of a public offering of 1,500,000 shares of its Class A common stock at a public offering price of $2.00 per share.

The release states that Cineverse granted the underwriter a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional 225,000 shares of common stock at the public offering price, less underwriting discounts and commissions. It also states that the gross proceeds from the offering are expected to be approximately $3.0 million before underwriting discounts, commissions, and other offering expenses.

The article says the offering is expected to close on February 17, 2026, subject to the satisfaction of customary closing conditions. Public reports also noted participation by Cineverse management in the offering.

This is the pricing-stage follow-up to the earlier proposal and provides a clear expected close date that supersedes the earlier undetermined timing.
