## Article

NEW YORK, Feb. 18, 2026 -- The OLB Group, Inc. announced that it entered into a securities purchase agreement with an institutional investor for the purchase and sale of 2,857,142 shares of common stock, or pre-funded warrants in lieu thereof, together with warrants to purchase up to 3,571,428 shares of common stock at a combined purchase price of $1.05 per share and accompanying warrants.

The release states that the warrants have an exercise price of $0.92 per share and are exercisable after effectiveness of the resale registration statement for the common stock underlying the warrants, with a term of five years from the effectiveness date of that resale registration statement. It also states that the Company agreed to reduce the exercise price of certain existing warrants held by the investor to $0.92 per share and extend the term of those existing warrants by three years.

The article says gross proceeds are expected to be approximately $3.0 million before commissions and expenses, and that the closing is expected to occur on or about February 19, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions. It also states that the securities are being sold in a private placement and that the Company entered into a registration rights agreement to file a resale registration statement covering the common stock and shares underlying the warrants.

This is an unusual private placement with immediate close timing, repriced existing warrants, and a later resale-registration trigger for warrant-related supply.
