## Article

SAN DIEGO, Jan. 29, 2026 -- Cibus, Inc. announced the pricing of an underwritten public offering of 13,333,333 shares of its Class A common stock at a public offering price of $1.50 per share.

The Company granted the underwriter a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional 1,999,999 shares to cover over-allotments. All shares to be sold in the offering are to be sold by the Company.

Gross proceeds are expected to be approximately $20.0 million before fees and expenses. The offering is expected to close on January 30, 2026, subject to the satisfaction of customary closing conditions.

The offering is being made pursuant to an effective shelf registration statement on Form S-3 and a prospectus supplement describing the terms of the offering will be filed with the SEC.
