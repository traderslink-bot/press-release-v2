## Article

TORONTO, March 20, 2026 -- MDA Space Ltd. announced that, in connection with its recently completed marketed public offering of common shares, the underwriters exercised their over-allotment option to purchase an additional 1,344,071 common shares at the public offering price of US$30.50 per share.

The release states that the option exercise results in additional aggregate gross proceeds to the Company of approximately US$41 million and increases the total gross proceeds of the offering to approximately US$341 million.

The Company says it intends to use the net proceeds of the offering to pursue growth strategies, support customer expansion, and for other strategic opportunities, with a portion also potentially used for general corporate purposes and repayment of amounts outstanding under existing credit facilities.

This is not a new proposal-stage financing. It is a post-IPO state update tied to exercise of an embedded underwriter option within an already completed public offering structure.
