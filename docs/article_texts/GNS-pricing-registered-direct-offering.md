## Article

Genius Group announced it entered into a securities purchase agreement with certain investors, including American Ventures LLC as lead investor, for the purchase and sale of 21,621,621 ordinary shares or pre funded warrants in a registered direct offering at a public offering price of $0.37 per share.

D. Boral Capital LLC is acting as the exclusive placement agent for the offering.

The company said the closing is expected to occur on or about April 16, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions, and that it expects aggregate gross proceeds of about $8 million before fees and expenses.

Genius said part of the net proceeds will fund an acquisition related to Jewel Financial and the remainder will support working capital and general corporate purposes.

The ordinary shares or pre funded warrants are being offered pursuant to an effective shelf registration statement on Form F-3, and the company said a prospectus supplement describing the registered direct offering will be filed with the SEC.
