Structure hint: private placement with resale registration commitment

## Article

Adlai Nortye announced that it entered into a securities purchase agreement for a private placement financing.

The Company stated that the private placement is expected to close on April 17, 2026, subject to the satisfaction of customary closing conditions.

The release also states that the securities sold in the private placement have not been registered under the Securities Act and may not be offered or sold in the United States absent registration or an applicable exemption. The Company agreed to file a registration statement for the resale of the securities sold in the private placement.

This is a useful split-timing example because issuance timing is tied to the expected close date, while public resale timing still depends on the later registration path described in the article.
