## Article

AUSTIN, Texas, Feb. 21, 2026 -- Ideal Power Inc. announced a proposed public offering of shares of its common stock.

The release states that all shares in the proposed offering will be sold by the Company. It also states that the offering is subject to market conditions and other factors, and that there can be no assurance as to whether or when the offering may be completed, or as to the actual size or terms of the offering.

The Company says it intends to use the net proceeds to advance commercialization of B-TRAN®, including customer design-ins, custom development programs, an initial production ramp with strategic partners, and for general corporate and working capital purposes. The offering is described as being made pursuant to an effective shelf registration statement on Form S-3 and may be offered only by means of a prospectus supplement and accompanying base prospectus.

This is a clean proposal-stage offering with no final pricing or closing date, so timing remains undetermined until a later pricing or closing update appears.
