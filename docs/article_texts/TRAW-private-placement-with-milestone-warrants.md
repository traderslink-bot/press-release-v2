## Article

Traws Pharma said it entered into a securities purchase agreement with new and existing institutional and accredited investors for a PIPE financing expected to provide about $10.0 million in gross proceeds at closing.

The company said the financing could provide up to $50 million of additional gross proceeds from milestone based and three year warrants.

The April 2026 financing consists of upfront common stock or pre funded warrants, a Series A warrant tied to MHRA approval to conduct a human challenge trial in the UK, a Series B warrant tied to both stockholder approval and challenge trial data, and a Series C warrant with a three year term that also requires stockholder approval before exercise.

Traws said the PIPE was priced at the market under Nasdaq rules, with each warrant exercise price equal to the deal price.

The financing was completed on April 15, 2026, with funding expected April 16, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions.

The PR says the securities were sold in a private transaction and were not registered under the Securities Act. Traws also agreed under a registration rights agreement to file a resale registration statement covering the common stock and the shares underlying the warrants and any pre funded warrants.
