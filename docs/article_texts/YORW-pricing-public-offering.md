## Article

The York Water Company announced the pricing of its previously announced underwritten public offering of common stock.

York Water priced 1,521,739 shares of common stock at $28.50 per share for aggregate gross proceeds of about $43 million before underwriting discounts and commissions.

The company also granted the underwriters a 30 day option to purchase up to 228,261 additional shares at the public offering price, less underwriting discounts and commissions.

York Water said it intends to use the net proceeds for general corporate purposes, including its capital investment program, repayment of outstanding indebtedness, and potential acquisitions.

The offering is being made pursuant to an effective shelf registration statement on Form S-3, and the company said the sale may be made only by means of a prospectus supplement and accompanying prospectus.
