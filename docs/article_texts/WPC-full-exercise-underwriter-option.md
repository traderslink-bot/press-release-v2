## Article

NEW YORK, Feb. 25, 2026 -- W. P. Carey Inc. announced that, in connection with its previously announced public offering of common stock offered on a forward basis, the full additional share option was exercised.

The release states that the option exercise closed on February 24, 2026. After giving effect to the option exercise, gross proceeds from the offering total $496.8 million.

The article also states that the Company expects any net proceeds received upon settlement of the forward sale agreements, or from any shares sold in lieu of the forward purchasers, to be used for future investments, debt repayment, and general corporate purposes.

This is a post-closing update tied to the earlier forward-sale offering and increases the total size of the already announced transaction.
