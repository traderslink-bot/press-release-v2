## Article

MOUNTAIN VIEW, Calif., March 16, 2026 -- Cyngn Inc. announced that it entered into definitive agreements in a registered direct offering for the purchase and sale of approximately $9.65 million of common stock and pre-funded warrants at a price of $1.93 per share of common stock.

The release states that one institutional investor acquired greater than ninety percent of the offering, with a high-net-worth individual purchasing the remainder. It also states that for each pre-funded unit sold in the offering, the number of shares of common stock in the offering will be decreased on a one-for-one basis.

The Company says aggregate gross proceeds are expected to be approximately $9.65 million and that the transaction is expected to close on or about March 17, 2026, subject to the satisfaction of customary closing conditions. The registered direct offering is described as being made pursuant to an effective shelf registration statement, with a final prospectus supplement and accompanying prospectus to be filed with the SEC.

This is a priced registered direct with a clear near-term close date and a mixed common-stock / pre-funded-warrant structure.
