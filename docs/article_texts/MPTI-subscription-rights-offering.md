## Article

ORLANDO, Fla., March 18, 2026 -- M-tron Industries, Inc. announced a subscription rights offering intended to raise capital for acquisitions, strategic investments, internal expansion, and other corporate purposes tied to defense sector demand.

The release states that the rights offering has a March 27, 2026 record date, that the rights are expected to begin regular-way trading on March 31, 2026 under the symbol MPTI RT, and that the rights are currently expected to expire at 5:00 p.m. Eastern Time on April 15, 2026, unless extended. It also states that five rights can be exercised to purchase one share of common stock at a subscription price that has not yet been determined.

The Company says eligible record-date holders who fully exercise their basic subscription rights may also participate in an oversubscription privilege for unsubscribed shares, subject to proration if aggregate subscriptions exceed the amount offered. The release further states that the rights agreement and rights certificate are expected to be filed with the SEC on or about March 30, 2026, and that the Company expects to file a prospectus supplement under its existing shelf registration statement covering the rights and underlying shares.

This is a special rights-offering structure with stated record and expiration dates, but it still depends on follow-on filing materials and final subscription pricing mechanics.
