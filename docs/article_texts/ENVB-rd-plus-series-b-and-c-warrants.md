## Article

CAMBRIDGE, Mass., April 16, 2026 -- Enveric Biosciences, Inc. announced that it entered into definitive agreements with institutional investors for a registered direct offering of common stock or pre-funded warrants and a concurrent private placement of Series B warrants and Series C warrants.

The Series B warrants and Series C warrants will each have an exercise price tied to the offering price and will be exercisable immediately upon issuance. The Company stated that no assurance can be given that any of the warrants will be exercised.

The offering is expected to close on or about April 17, 2026, subject to satisfaction of customary closing conditions.

The shares of common stock or pre-funded warrants are being offered pursuant to an effective registration statement. The private placement securities and the shares underlying them were described as not registered under the Securities Act and not offerable or saleable absent an effective registration statement or an applicable exemption. The Company also agreed to file resale registration statement(s) covering the resale of the ordinary shares issuable upon exercise of the warrants.
