## Article

SEATTLE, April 16, 2026 -- Achieve Life Sciences, Inc. announced a private placement financing that includes upfront proceeds and additional potential proceeds from accompanying warrants.

The release states that the private placement is expected to close on April 17, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions. It also states that the securities sold in the private placement have not been registered under the Securities Act and may not be offered or sold absent registration or an applicable exemption, and that the Company agreed to file a resale registration statement within 30 days of closing.

The accompanying warrants are described as exercisable any time after issuance, but their expiration is tied to the later of the twentieth business day following public announcement of FDA approval or company notice of FDA approval, with additional extension mechanics if stockholder approval is required to increase authorized shares.

This creates a mixed timing structure where the financing can close in the near term, but part of the future supply path depends on milestone language, resale registration, and potentially stockholder approval.
