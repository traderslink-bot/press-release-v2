## Article

GAINESVILLE, Texas, Feb. 20, 2026 -- Select Water Solutions, Inc. announced the pricing of a public offering of common stock.

The release states that the offering is expected to close on February 23, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions. It also states that the transaction is being handled by a syndicate led by Citigroup, Piper Sandler, and Raymond James, with several additional co-managers participating in the offering.

The article says the securities are being offered only by means of a prospectus and final prospectus supplement meeting the requirements of the Securities Act. This is a standard pricing-stage financing update with a stated expected close date.

This is a straightforward priced public offering and fits the delayed-until-close family rather than an immediate issuance event.
