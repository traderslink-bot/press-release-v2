## Article

SHENZHEN, China, March 17, 2026 -- Meiwu Technology Co Ltd announced a registered direct offering of ordinary shares.

The release states that investors agreed to purchase 6,999,996 ordinary shares at $2.00 per share. It also states that the Company agreed to issue warrants to purchase up to 6,999,996 ordinary shares at an exercise price of $2.00 per share, with a one-year term from issuance.

The Company says aggregate gross proceeds are expected to be approximately $14 million and that the transaction is expected to close on or about March 18, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions. The offering is described as being made under an effective Form F-3 shelf registration statement, with a final prospectus supplement and accompanying prospectus to be filed with the SEC.

This is a priced registered direct with a near-term close date and added warrant supply beyond the initial share sale.
