## Article

Foremost Clean Energy said it entered into a bought deal private placement with Canaccord Genuity for 1,618,000 units at C$3.40 per unit for aggregate gross proceeds of about C$5.5 million.

Each unit includes one common share and one half of one common share purchase warrant. Each whole warrant is exercisable for 24 months from the closing date at C$4.40 per share.

The company said its largest shareholder, Denison Mines, may participate under an investor rights agreement to maintain ownership up to about 19.95 percent after closing.

Foremost also granted the underwriter an option exercisable up to 48 hours before closing to purchase up to 242,700 additional units, which would add about C$825,180 of gross proceeds if fully exercised.

The offering is expected to close on or about April 7, 2026, subject to conditions including exchange and Nasdaq approvals.

The PR also says the securities have not been and will not be registered under the U.S. Securities Act and may not be offered or sold in the United States absent registration or an available exemption.
