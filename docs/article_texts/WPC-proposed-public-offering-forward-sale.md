## Article

NEW YORK, Feb. 17, 2026 -- W. P. Carey Inc. announced the commencement of an underwritten public offering of 6,000,000 shares of common stock, offered on a forward basis in connection with forward sale agreements.

The release states that the underwriters were granted a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional 900,000 shares of common stock. It also states that the Company expects to enter into forward sale agreements with Bank of America, N.A. and JPMorgan Chase Bank, National Association, and that the forward purchasers are expected to borrow shares from third parties and sell them to the underwriters.

The article says that, subject to the Company's right to elect cash or net share settlement, the Company would issue and deliver shares upon physical settlement of the forward sale agreements on one or more dates specified by the Company occurring no later than approximately 24 months from the date of the prospectus supplement. The Company expects to receive proceeds upon one or more such physical settlements within approximately 24 months.

This is not a plain same-day share issuance structure. It is a forward-sale launch event where ultimate company issuance timing depends on later settlement mechanics.
