## Article

SHANGHAI, March 19, 2026 -- U Power Limited announced the pricing of an underwritten public offering of 13,360,000 units on a firm commitment basis at a price to the public of $0.449 per unit.

The release states that each unit consists of one Class A ordinary share and one Class A warrant. It also states that the gross proceeds from the offering, before discounts, expenses, and any warrant exercise proceeds, are expected to be approximately $6.0 million.

The Company says the closing of the offering is expected to occur on or about March 20, 2026, subject to the satisfaction of customary closing conditions. The securities are described as being offered pursuant to an effective Form F-1 registration statement that was declared effective by the SEC on March 18, 2026.

This is a priced public offering with a near-term expected close date and a mixed unit structure that includes warrants beyond the initial share issuance.
