## Article

The York Water Company announced a proposed public offering of its common stock, with all shares being offered by the company.

York Water also said it intends to grant the underwriters a 30 day option to purchase additional shares of common stock.

The company said it expects to use the net proceeds for general corporate purposes, including its capital investment program, repayment of outstanding indebtedness, and potential acquisitions.

The offering is subject to market and other customary closing conditions, and the company said it cannot assure investors whether or when the offering may be completed.

The proposed offering is being made pursuant to an effective shelf registration statement on Form S-3, and the PR says the sale may be made only by means of a prospectus supplement and accompanying prospectus.
