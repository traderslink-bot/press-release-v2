## Article

NEW YORK, Feb. 19, 2026 -- W. P. Carey Inc. announced the closing of its previously announced underwritten public offering of 6,000,000 shares of common stock, offered on a forward basis in connection with forward sale agreements.

The release states that the gross proceeds from the offering are $432 million. It also states that the underwriters had been granted a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional 900,000 shares of common stock.

This is a post-pricing closing update rather than a new proposal-stage event. Because the offering was conducted on a forward basis, the structure still carries forward-sale settlement mechanics even after the closing announcement.
