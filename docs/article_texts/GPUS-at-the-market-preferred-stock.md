## Article

LAS VEGAS, Feb. 13, 2026 -- Hyperscale Data, Inc. announced that it established an at-the-market equity offering program under which it may sell, from time to time, shares of its 13% Series D Cumulative Redeemable Perpetual Preferred Stock for aggregate gross proceeds of up to approximately $35.4 million.

The release states that the shares will be offered through Wilson-Davis & Co., Inc. as sales agent. It explains that sales may occur in ordinary brokers' transactions on the NYSE American or otherwise at market prices prevailing at the time of sale or as agreed with the agent.

The Company says it intends to use a majority of any net proceeds to acquire Bitcoin and potentially precious metals, with a smaller amount for working capital and general corporate purposes. The release also says the preferred stock is being offered under an effective shelf registration statement and may be offered only by means of a prospectus supplement and accompanying prospectus.

This is a company-controlled ATM structure rather than a single priced closing event, so the timing is open-ended and depends on future sales activity.
