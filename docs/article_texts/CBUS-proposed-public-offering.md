## Article

SAN DIEGO, Jan. 28, 2026 -- Cibus, Inc. announced that it intends to offer shares of its Class A common stock in an underwritten public offering.

Cibus also expects to grant the underwriter a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional 15% of the shares offered on the same terms and conditions.

The offering is subject to market and other conditions, and there can be no assurance as to whether or when the offering may be completed or as to the actual size or terms of the offering.

The release states that the securities will be offered pursuant to an effective shelf registration statement on Form S-3 and that a prospectus supplement describing the terms of the offering will be filed with the SEC.
